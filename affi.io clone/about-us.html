About Affi.io

Affi.io help finds you in which platform e-retailers have their affiliate program.

It was create for my personal use, I have a lot of affiliate program to manage in many countries, it's difficult to follow which program are already accepted or in pending with an Excel file...
With Affi.io you can find e-retailers by keyword or by affiliate program, you can tag each affiliate program if it's accepted, in pending or refused and comment to share your experience.

If you are an affiliate platform and you want help us to add your pograms, please contact us.

If you need data please don't scrap it and ask us.
